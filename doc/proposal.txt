Entrepreneur & Investor
Collaboration Platform
Project Title
This platform is designed to connect entrepreneurs with potential
investors through secure profiles. Both parties can communicate,
send proposals, and engage in contract-based investment using
smart verification. The aim is to reduce fraud and strengthen
professional business connections.
Introduction
Objective
To develop a smart and secure web platform that allows investors and
entrepreneurs to connect easily and build professional business
relationships.
Provide a user-friendly interface where entrepreneurs can present
their business ideas and investors can review them smoothly.
Offer a verified business listing system to reduce fraud and increase
trust between both parties.
Ensure the security and privacy of user data through strong
authentication, profile verification, and protected contract signing.
Enable transparent communication through features like chat, live
pitch streaming, progress updates, and milestone tracking.
Support cloud-based services for scalability, high availability, and
fast access from anywhere.
Problem Description
Current business funding platforms lack transparency, secure
verification, and effective communication between investors and
entrepreneurs. Investors often find it hard to trust new startups,
while entrepreneurs struggle to reach genuine investors. This project
provides a secure platform where both parties can connect, verify
profiles, communicate, sign digital agreements, and easily track
business progress.
Methodology
The project will follow an iterative process, beginning with
requirements gathering from investors and entrepreneurs, then moving
through interface design, backend development, and feature
integration. Continuous testing will ensure smooth communication and
investment tracking. Using Agile methodology, the system will remain
flexible for quick updates based on user feedback. After successful
testing, the platform will be deployed on a secure, scalable cloud
environment.
The project focuses on creating an online system where entrepreneurs
can present their business ideas, request funding, and communicate
professionally; meanwhile, investors can browse proposals, analyze
opportunities, and manage funding interests using filters and
verification tools.
Project Scope
The idea is feasible because:
Startups are increasing rapidly
Investors need structured deal-flow
Market lacks a transparent communication channel
Both technical and economic feasibility are strong, as the platform
can be built using widely available frameworks.
Feasibility Study
This platform can be applied in:
Business incubation centers
Investment networking events
University startup programs
Financial advisory platforms
It can also help students build entrepreneurial portfolios.
